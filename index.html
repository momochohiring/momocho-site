Our Story

Founded by Chef Eric Williams in 2006, Momocho pioneered the 'Mod Mex' movement— a high-energy take on traditional Mexican cuisine. For 20 years, we’ve been pushing boundaries with modern flair, unique twists, and interesting flavors that define the spirit of Ohio City.
